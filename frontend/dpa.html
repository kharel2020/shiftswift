HR Data Processing Addendum (outline)

Draft outline for customer contracts. Review with a UK solicitor before use as a binding agreement.

ShiftSwift HR · Datasoftware Analytics Ltd (Company No. 14568900)

1. Roles

  • Customer: Data Controller (employer)
  • Provider / Processor: Datasoftware Analytics Ltd, 235 Charlbury Road, Nottingham, NG8 1NF — trading as ShiftSwift HR

2. Subject matter and duration

Processing employee personal data for HR administration for the term of the MSA plus the agreed deletion period.

3. Nature and purpose

Storage and display of employee records, rotas, payroll data, and compliance artefacts (RTW PDFs, absence alerts, SMS change logs).

4. Categories of data subjects

Customer’s employees, workers, and applicants.

5. Categories of personal data

Identity, contact, employment, salary, disciplinary, right-to-work, and sponsorship status.

6. Data silo / segregation

Provider operates ShiftSwift HR on infrastructure logically and physically segregated from unrelated commerce systems. Employee HR data is stored in dedicated HR tenant databases. HR records are not commingled with EPOS sales data, order logs, or payment card data.

7. Sub-processors

Hosting provider, email provider, and Stripe (billing metadata only — not employee HR files).

8. Security measures

  • Encryption in transit (TLS)
  • Access control, RBAC, tenant isolation
  • Audit logging

9. Data subject requests

Provider assists the Customer in responding to data subject requests within agreed timeframes.

10. Breach notification

Provider notifies Customer without undue delay after becoming aware of a personal data breach affecting HR data.

11. Deletion / return

On termination, delete or return HR data within the agreed period except where legal retention applies.
