Pricing FAQ

Is HMRC RTI included?

HMRC RTI submissions are included when you subscribe to a payroll add-on plan. The HR platform covers records, rotas, and compliance modules.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — B2B subscriptions run month-to-month or annually per your order form. See our payment terms for notice periods.

Does the trial need a card?

You can start a 14-day trial without entering card details locally. Stripe billing is provisioned at signup when configured for production.

Can I add payroll later?

Yes. Subscribe to HR only and add a payroll band from admin billing whenever you are ready — pricing is separate from the platform plan.
