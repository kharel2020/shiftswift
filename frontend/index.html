Frequently asked questions

Is HMRC RTI included in all plans?

RTI submissions are available as a payroll add-on, priced separately by headcount. The HR platform covers employee records, rotas, right-to-work, and compliance modules. You can subscribe to HR only and add payroll whenever you are ready.

Do I need to enter card details to start a trial?

No. Your 14-day free trial starts immediately. No payment details are required. At the end of your trial, you choose the plan that suits you.

Can I add payroll later?

Yes. Start on any HR plan and activate a payroll add-on from your billing settings at any time. Payroll is priced separately by staff headcount.

Does ShiftSwift HR cover sponsor licence compliance?

Yes. ShiftSwift HR includes day-9 absence alerts, SMS change reporting, right-to-work document management, and Home Office audit export packs, covering the core duties of a UK sponsor licence holder.

Is my data stored in the UK?

Yes. All data is hosted on UK servers. ShiftSwift HR is GDPR compliant and operated by Datasoftware Analytics Ltd, a UK-registered company.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time. Annual plans run to the end of the billing period. See our payment terms for full details.

Do you support multi-site businesses?

Yes. Plans are priced per site, so you add a plan for each location. A multi-site dashboard is available on Scale and Enterprise plans, giving you a consolidated view across all sites.
