Frequently asked questions

How does per-head billing work?

Each plan has a small monthly base fee plus a per-active-employee charge. Only employees with an active, onboarding, or suspended status count — managers and HR admins are free. When headcount grows, your bill rises predictably until you hit the plan's monthly cap; above that, you pay the cap amount only.

Are you in early access?

Yes. The product is live for trials and pilot customers on UK-hosted infrastructure, but we are still completing our go-live checklist — Stripe live billing, solicitor-reviewed contracts, and our first named case study. Book a demo and judge the product yourself rather than relying on template marketing quotes.

Do you run payroll and HMRC RTI?

No — and that is intentional. Most small UK businesses already use BrightPay, Xero, or a payroll bureau. ShiftSwift HR handles employee records and UK compliance, then exports CSV files you import into your payroll software. RTI, payslips, and P45s stay where they belong.

Do I need to enter card details to start a trial?

No. Your 14-day free trial starts immediately. No payment details are required. At the end of your trial, you choose the plan that suits you.

How do I connect BrightPay or Xero?

Open Payroll export in your admin console, download the employee CSV, and import it into BrightPay or add employees in Xero. A step-by-step BrightPay guide is included. Optional hours CSV is available if you use Time punch.

Does ShiftSwift HR cover sponsor licence compliance?

Yes. ShiftSwift HR includes day-9 absence alerts, SMS change reporting, right-to-work document management, and Home Office audit export packs, covering the core duties of a UK sponsor licence holder.

Is my data stored in the UK?

Yes. All data is hosted on UK servers. ShiftSwift HR is GDPR compliant and operated by Datasoftware Analytics Ltd, a UK-registered company.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time and run to the end of the billing period. See our payment terms for full details.

Do you support multi-site businesses?

Yes. Plans are priced per site — add a subscription for each location. The Multi-site plan includes a consolidated dashboard across locations; Enterprise covers larger rollouts and bespoke SLAs.
