Time clock

Clock in and out when you are at an assigned work site. Location access is required.

For the best experience on your phone, use the Time Clock app and add it to your home screen (see below).

Install Time Clock on your phone

There is no App Store or Play Store download. Open Time Clock on your phone once, then add it to your home screen.

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

  1. Open Time Clock in Safari.
  2. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen → Add.

Android (Chrome)

  1. Open Time Clock in Chrome.
  2. Tap Install app or the menu → Add to Home screen.

Allow location when prompted — you must be at your work site to clock in.
