      Employment contracts

      Contracts between your business and employees. Templates are curated from ACAS guidance and updated when UK employment law changes — apply platform updates in HR Templates.

      HR Templates ACAS templates →
      Choose template → Generate → Employee signs → Stored on file

      Platform sync: Employment templates live in the HR template catalog (category: contracts). When UK law changes, we publish a new platform version — tenants with custom edits see “Update available” in HR Templates.

      How it works

      1. Customise templates in HR Templates (Markdown) — or use platform defaults.
      2. Generate below — merges employee + business details into the contract.
      3. Send for e-signature — employee receives a link; they sign in the browser (HTML, not PDF).
      4. Signed copy is saved automatically to the employee document store.

      Generate employment contract

      Prefills employee and employer details from the employee profile and business settings. Employee must have an email address before sending for signature.
