Compliance checklist

Internal operator guide for Datasoftware Analytics Ltd. Not legal advice — review with a UK solicitor.

Last updated: 10 June 2026

1. ICO & UK GDPR

  • ICO registration live and fee renewed annually
  • Privacy policy published (includes Time Clock GPS)
  • Cookie policy + consent banner on all site pages
  • Record of Processing Activities (ROPA) written
  • Data breach playbook (notify customers within 72 hours)
  • SAR / erasure process owned by legal@datasoftwareanalytics.co.uk
  • Customer offboarding: export + delete runbook

2. Customer contracts

  • MSA + DPA reviewed by UK solicitor
  • Signed DPA with each paying customer
  • Sub-processor list maintained (hosting, email, Stripe)
  • Marketing claims match shipped product

3. Employee data (processor)

  • Customers provide employee privacy notices (mention HR system + GPS punch)
  • Time punch location covered in privacy policy and DPA
  • RTW documents included in backups
  • DPIA considered for GPS + immigration data

4. Security & operations

  • HTTPS on www, app, and api
  • Daily Postgres backups + tested restore
  • Monthly security audit script
  • MFA on company email and cloud admin

5. What you do not need today

Home Office registration (employers hold sponsor duties), FCA authorisation, or a separate UK software compliance register — unless you expand into regulated activities.
