Payroll export · BrightPay & Xero

Export HR data to your payroll software

ShiftSwift HR handles employee records and UK compliance. Your accountant or payroll bureau runs HMRC RTI in BrightPay, Xero, or similar — we export the employee data they need.

1. Download employee CSV

In admin, open Payroll export and click Download employee CSV. Active and onboarding staff are included with NI number, start date, salary, and contact details.

2. Import into BrightPay

  1. Open BrightPay for your employer.
  2. Go to Employees → import/add employees.
  3. Choose CSV import and map columns: Forename, Surname, NI Number, Date of Birth, Start Date, etc.
  4. Review each employee before saving — keep ShiftSwift as your master HR record.

3. Optional: hours CSV

If you use Time punch, download Hours CSV for the pay period and enter totals in BrightPay or share with your bureau.

4. Xero Payroll

Add employees in Xero using the same CSV fields, or copy details manually. Run pay runs and RTI submissions inside Xero — not in ShiftSwift HR.
