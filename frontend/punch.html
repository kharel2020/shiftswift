How to install on your phone

There is no App Store download. Add this page to your home screen once — then open the Time Clock icon to clock in at work.

iPhone / iPad

  1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome).
  2. Tap Share (square with arrow).
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen, then Add.

Android

  1. Open this page in Chrome.
  2. Tap Install app (banner or ⋮ menu).
  3. Confirm Add to Home screen.

When you clock in or out outdoors, allow location when your phone asks. Indoors, scan the premises QR code instead.
