# B2B payment terms — ShiftSwift HR

**Publish on your website and link from checkout. Solicitor review recommended.**

## Subscriptions

ShiftSwift HR subscriptions are sold **business-to-business (B2B)** to companies, partnerships, and sole traders operating restaurants, cafés, pubs, and similar hospitality businesses.

## VAT

Where applicable, **UK VAT at 20%** is calculated and displayed on invoices via Stripe Tax. Customers may provide a UK or EU VAT registration number at checkout for validation.

## Billing cycle

- **Monthly plans:** charged in advance each month
- **Annual plans:** charged in advance for 12 months
- Invoices are issued electronically to the billing email on the account

## No cooling-off period (B2B)

Unlike consumer contracts, **B2B software subscriptions are not subject to the 14-day consumer cooling-off period** under UK law. By completing checkout, the Customer confirms it is purchasing for business purposes.

## Refunds

**All subscription fees are non-refundable**, including:

- Partial months or unused periods
- Failure to use the software
- Customer change of mind

Exceptions only where:

- Required by mandatory applicable law, or
- Provider fails to provide the service for a sustained period per the MSA SLA (credit or pro-rata remedy at Provider's discretion)

## Cancellation

- Customer may cancel at end of current billing period via the billing portal
- Access continues until period end; no refund for remaining days
- Data export available before deletion per DPA

## Failed payments

- Stripe will retry failed card charges
- Provider may suspend access after [7–14 days] of non-payment
